mutation_details(mutations)

outbreak_data.mutation_details(mutations, server='api.outbreak.info')

Returns details of a mutation.

Arguments:
mutations:

(Required). Comma separated list of mutations.

return:

A pandas dataframe.

Example usage:

#Get info on mutation 'orf1b:p314l'
df = outbreak_data.mutation_details('orf1b:p314l')
print(df)
