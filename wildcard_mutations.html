wildcard_mutations(name)

outbreak_data.wildcard_mutations(name, server='api.outbreak.info')

Match mutations using wildcards.

Arguments:
name:

(Required) A string. Must use * at end of string. Supports wildcards. (Example: s:e484*)

return:

A pandas dataframe.
