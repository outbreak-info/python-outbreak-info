growth_rates(lineage, location)

outbreak_data.growth_rates(lineage, location='Global')

Returns the growth rate score for a given lineage in a given location.

Arguments:
lineage:

(Required) A string.

location:

(Required. Default: ‘Global’) A list or string. Separate multiple locations with “,”

return:

A pandas dataframe.
